WEATHRING WITH YOU

STORY

"Weathering with You" follows the story of Hodaka, a high school runaway who moves to Tokyo and encounters Hina, a girl with the ability to control the weather. As Tokyo faces incessant rain, Hina's ability to bring sunshine brings hope and joy to people's lives. Hodaka and Hina start a business together, offering their services to bring good weather to those who need it, but soon they discover the toll this power takes on Hina. As their bond deepens, they face difficult choices between personal desires and the greater good. The film explores themes of love, sacrifice, and the consequences of altering nature's course in a beautifully animated and emotionally resonant narrative.
